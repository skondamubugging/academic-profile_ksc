About

Kondamudi Srichandan works across electromagnetic launcher systems, solar photovoltaic engineering, and solar-powered reverse osmosis desalination, with a publication record spanning IEEE, Springer, Elsevier, and AIP venues. His research combines electromagnetic device design (multipole, quadrupole, and coil-based launchers) with sustainable-energy system evaluation for coastal and water-stressed regions of India.

He has supervised Ph.D. and postgraduate research to completion, holds seven published patents across solar, IoT-robotics, and electromagnetic-launch domains, and has organized and attended workshops and faculty development programmes spanning power systems, control engineering, and emerging EV technologies.
